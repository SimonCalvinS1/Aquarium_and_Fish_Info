This page shows some measures to be followed to maintain good fish care. As a new fish parent, it is confusing to take care of fishes at home sometimes. To avoid this, these below tips can be followed, which maintains the good health of the fishes.


  1. You should change about 15 to 25% of your aquarium water every one to two weeks.
  2. When you change your aquarium water, you should clean the rest of the tank as well.
  3. The percentage of water and frequency of water changes depend on the volume of fish and plant life in your tank.
  4. Changing the water in your tank is the most important maintenance you can perform on your aquarium, so following a regular schedule will greatly benefit the health and quality of life of your marine life.
  5. Ensure all pumps, filters, and lights are working properly.
  6. Observe fishes for negative side effects. If any side effects are evident, test the water immediately. If the water is fine but the fish remain ill, check the filters and pumps for any malfunctions.
  7. Remove excess food from the tank.
  8. Check the water temperature. Ensure that the temperature of your tank is suitable for all of your marine life.
